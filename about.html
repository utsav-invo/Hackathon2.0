Our Mission

The Medicine Availability Tracker is designed to help patients and healthcare providers quickly find nearby pharmacies and hospitals with the required medicine stock. This tool aims to reduce travel time and ensure timely access to essential medications.

We are a dedicated team of BCA students committed to leveraging technology for better healthcare outcomes. Our project combines web development, data management, and user-friendly design to create a seamless experience for users.

Features

  • Real-time search for medicine availability
  • Interactive map integration
  • Admin panel for data management
  • Responsive design for all devices
